About

I love math, code, and dogs. (And a lot of other stuff, but it's too much to put here.)

I'm Anthony Pizzimenti, a junior at the University of Iowa. I study mathematics and computer science, with interests in graph theory, algebraic combinatorics, and algorithm design. I am also a research partner with the Metric Geometry and Gerrymandering Group. Working with Dr. Moon Duchin, we explore the problems of gerrymandering from a mathematical perspective.
