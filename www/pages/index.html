Me

I am Anthony Pizzimenti. I am a student, developer, tutor, and hockey player.

I attend the University of Iowa and am working toward Computer Science and Mathematics degrees, as well as a certificate in Large Data Analysis. I work as a full-stack software developer at the University of Iowa Information Technology services, and do quite a bit of open-source development on the side. I am also a tutor at the University, so see the contact page for more info there. Finally, I sit on the College of Liberal Arts and Sciences Student Technology Fees board, which decides how to parcel a ~$2m budget to each department.

I enjoy spending time with my family, my friends, and working on projects that help others. I consider myself involved in politics (local and state included) in an effort to build community and change things for the better. Aside from that, I'm a pretty good pool player and I can make a mean pasta dinner.

I've always dabbled in media creation, and I'll be starting up a podcast called Idaho City, Ohio in the 2017 Fall semester which talks about the happenings at the University and Iowa City. My favorite podcasts are anything from HeadGum, Crooked Media, and the fine people at NPR. A few favorite TV shows include The Office, Riverdale, Parks and Recreation, and The Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt.

Programming

My favorite languages are JavaScript [1] [2] and its flavors of Node and TypeScript, Python[1] [2], C, and Bash. Aside from my favorite languages, I have done work in C#, C++, PHP, (too much) Java, and a hint of R. A very disparate set of languages, yes, but they are the ones I've chosen.

I have done quite a bit of open-source work in JavaScript and Python, which you can see on the projects page and on github. I plan on doing more open-source work related to networking and internet privacy this upcoming year.
